about

This site started because we wanted to move and we had a list of a few US cities in mind.

Some cities we have friends to ask, but others we don't have friends to ask, so we thought it would be a sweet idea to make a site with profiles of people explaining their lifestyles a bit to get a sense of what its like to live in that particular city.

We love traveling, it makes our world go round!
